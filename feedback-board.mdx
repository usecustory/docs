The feedback board gives your team one visible place to collect and review product feedback.

## What the board is for

Use the board when you want to reduce scattered requests and give customers or teammates a clearer way to:

- Report bugs
- Request features
- Ask for integrations
- Add other product feedback

## Available feedback types

Custory supports these main categories:

- Feature requests
- Bug reports
- Integration requests
- Other feedback

## Review modes

You can work with feedback in more than one way.

- Use the board-style view for quick browsing by category
- Use the list-style workflow for filtering, systematic review, and status checks

## Signals on each post

Feedback posts can include:

- Likes
- Subscriptions
- Importance voting
- Comments and discussion
- Status

That helps your team see not only what was said, but also how much support or urgency exists around it.

## Why it matters

The board is useful when product signal is repeated but fragmented. It helps your team spot patterns sooner and gives people a visible place to understand whether something was already reported or picked up.
