A workspace is where your team works together in Custory.

It contains journeys, personas, integrations, members, and settings.

## Create a clear workspace

Use a workspace for one company, product, or product area.

Examples:

- Acme
- Acme Growth
- Acme Support Experience

Keep the name recognizable so invited teammates know they are in the right place.

## Invite teammates

Invite people who can add or use customer context.

Good teammates to invite:

- Product managers
- CX and support leads
- Designers
- Founders
- Customer success
- Sales leaders
- Engineers who need customer context for product work

## Keep ownership clear

For important journeys and opportunities, make sure someone owns the next step.

Ownership does not need to be heavy. It just answers:

- Who will look at this?
- Who will decide what happens next?
- Who will keep the item updated?

## Workspace habits

Helpful habits:

- Review one key journey every week
- Add evidence when a problem repeats
- Link product work back to the related opportunity
- Clean up old items during planning
- Invite support and CX early, not after decisions are made
