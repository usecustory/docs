## Who these docs are for

These docs are written for smart, busy teams that want faster clarity, not a bigger process.

That usually means:

- founders
- product leads
- customer success leads
- early operators
- small cross-functional teams

You do not need to be an expert in customer journey mapping to get value from Custory.
