Custory gives your team one shared system for understanding the customer journey and deciding what to improve next.

Instead of splitting context across chat, issue trackers, docs, analytics, and whiteboards, you keep the journey, the evidence, the follow-up work, and the AI workflow connected in one workspace.

## Product areas

Custory is organized around a few connected workflows:

- **Workspace and access** for members, roles, invitations, join requests, and billing
- **Journeys** for the customer flow itself
- **Items** for evidence, problems, ideas, metrics, and decisions
- **Personas** for the customer lens behind each journey
- **Views** for narrative review, cleanup, filtering, and prioritization
- **AI** for drafting, analysis, edits, memory, and follow-up work
- **Automations** for scheduled and event-based workflows
- **Integrations** for chat, issue tracking, analytics, design, docs, and developer tools
