Custory uses AI to help with product and customer work.

The goal is not to replace your judgment. The goal is to remove blank-page work and help your team move faster with the context you already have.

## What AI can help with

Custory AI can help you:

- Draft journey stages
- Summarize customer evidence
- Rewrite unclear notes
- Suggest opportunities
- Turn a solution into a clearer build prompt
- Explain what changed in a journey
- Help set up automations in plain language

## Use AI where the work happens

Custory AI is designed to work close to the journey, step, or item you are editing.

That means the answer can use the surrounding context instead of asking you to explain everything again.

## Automations

Automations help keep the journey current.

Examples:

- Pull a weekly signal from an analytics tool
- Create follow-up work when a new opportunity is marked ready
- Send a team update when important journey context changes
- Draft a summary from new customer evidence

## Review before you use

Always review AI output before relying on it.

AI can help organize and draft, but your team should still decide:

- Is this true?
- Is the evidence strong enough?
- Is this the right priority?
- Is the next step clear?

<Warning>
  Do not paste private customer data into places where your team would not normally store it. Follow your company privacy rules.
</Warning>
