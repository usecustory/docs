---
title: "Glossary"
description: "Plain-language definitions for the main objects and workflows in Custory."
icon: "book"
---

## Automation

A workflow that runs on a schedule or in response to an event and then performs one or more follow-up actions.

## Insight

Something your team learned about the customer experience.

## Integration

A connection between Custory and another tool such as Slack, GitHub, Notion, PostHog, or Stripe.

## Item

A structured piece of context attached to a journey, such as a touchpoint, insight, opportunity, solution, or metric.

## Journey

A map of a customer flow that your team can review, edit, and use for decisions.

## MCP server

The workspace-scoped server that lets external AI clients connect to Custory through the Model Context Protocol.

## Metric

A number your team watches to understand whether an experience is improving or getting worse.

## Opportunity

A customer problem or improvement area worth acting on.

## Persona

A workspace-level customer profile that can be linked to one or more journeys.

## Solution

A proposed or planned change meant to address an opportunity.

## Stage

A major phase in a journey.

## Step

A specific customer moment inside a stage.

## Touchpoint

Something the customer sees, uses, receives, or interacts with.

## Version history

A timeline of journey snapshots that you can review, label, preview, or restore.

## Workspace

The shared home for a team’s journeys, personas, integrations, automations, members, and settings.
