## Automation

A repeatable action Custory can run to help keep work moving.

## Evidence

Proof that supports an insight, opportunity, or decision. This can be a quote, ticket, metric, recording, screenshot, or link.

## Insight

Something your team learned about the customer experience.

## Integration

A connection between Custory and another tool.

## Item

A piece of context attached to a journey step, such as an insight, opportunity, solution, metric, touchpoint, or evidence.

## Journey

A map of what customers go through.

## Metric

A number your team watches to understand whether something is improving or getting worse.

## Opportunity

A customer problem or improvement area worth considering.

## Persona

A simple profile of an important customer type.

## Solution

A possible fix or improvement for an opportunity.

## Stage

A larger part of a journey.

## Step

A specific customer moment inside a stage.

## Touchpoint

Something the customer sees, uses, reads, receives, or interacts with.

## Workspace

The shared home for your team's journeys, personas, integrations, and settings.
